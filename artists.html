Some artists I think deserve your attention

Plagueofgripes

Out of all three, I have been following Plagueofgripes the longest. He makes interesting art videos like the ones based around "trifusion" in which he combines three characters in different ways to create new designs. He also discusses aspects of art and character design often in his videos.



Crabby-draws

Crabby-draws is another artist on youtube I fine interesting and only found out about recently. His videos also discuss character design and while his uploads are few, the content so far suggests awesome uploads to come.



Mothmandraws

Mothmandraws is an artist with a special place in my heart. His painterly style is awesome to look at and he also created awesome artwork for the guys at Super Best Friends Play on youtube.
