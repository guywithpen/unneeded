Made in Abyss

This show is one of my favorites because of it's charming main characters, Reg and Riko, as well as the lore and mystery surrounding the dangerous abyss Riko enters to find her mother.



Patlabor

LADY COPS + GIANT ROBOTS + SUPLEXES= This wonderful show. In a setting where giant robots are used for dangerous work, many abuse these machines for criminal activity. Our protagonist patrols in her own robot equipped with a massive baton and pistol.
